The Defense Builders Directory lists 147 companies hand-selected to attend Merge Combinator's first Defense Tech Combine. This included our in-person competition, SigmaBlox (Tulsa, OK), where 83 companies competed and learned from Government and Industry SME. Scores, observations, and analytics are used by USG stakeholders as market research and source selection for government contracts. Cohort 25-1 spanned six national Critical Technology Areas: Applied Artificial Intelligence (AAI), Biomanufacturing (BIO), Contested Logistics Technologies (LOG), Quantum and Battlefield Information Dominance (Q-BID), Scaled Directed Energy (SCADE), and Scaled Hypersonics (SHY).
