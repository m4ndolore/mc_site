Kristen - Mission Portfolio Lead

An Army finance veteran and acquisition professional, Kristen spent nearly two decades navigating the intersection of defense budgeting, resource planning, and program execution across multiple Army commands. She managed budgets spanning O&M, Procurement, and RDT&E appropriations for major acquisition programs at PEO Aviation, PEO Combat Support, the Army Budget Office, and PEO Soldier, advising senior leaders on funding policy and resource management strategy.

She applies an honest fiscal lens to identify which capability gaps are real, funded, or neither—helping venture teams navigate the Valley of Death by mapping color-of-money strategies, POM positioning, SBIR cadence, and transition funding across the portfolio. Her organizing concept is red teaming acquisition: synthesizing what a government decision-maker actually needs to see and helping industry avoid the pitfalls that kill capability before it reaches the warfighter.

Camellia King-Stanley - Capability Decision Lead

Ms. Camillia King-Stanley has sixteen years of experience in government service, with a background in contracting and program management. Currently, Ms. King-Stanley is the Business Lead for the $2 billion Middle Tier Acquisition – Rapid Fielding of the Long Range Hypersonic program with responsibility for cost, schedule, and performance, ensuring alignment with DoD directives and strategic goals. Ms. King-Stanley received her Bachelor of Science degree from Howard University and her Master’s degree from The University of Southern Mississippi.
