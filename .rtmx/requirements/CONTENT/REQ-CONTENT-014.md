# REQ-CONTENT-014 — Named Testimonials from Real Founders/Operators

**Category:** CONTENT / PROOF  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Phase:** 12  
**Status:** BACKLOG  

## Problem

No testimonials with real names and faces. Quote carousel exists but lacks verifiable attribution. "Anonymous operator, INDOPACOM" doesn't build trust. Named quotes from real humans with photos, titles, and companies do.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Minimum 2-3 named testimonials from real Combine alumni or operator partners
2. Each includes: photo, full name, title/company, specific quote about MC experience
3. Quotes reference specific outcomes (not generic praise)
4. Testimonials appear on homepage

## Dependencies

Requires outreach to Combine alumni and operators for permission. Candidate companies: Ulysses (Series A), any of the 21 funded-for-follow-on companies.
