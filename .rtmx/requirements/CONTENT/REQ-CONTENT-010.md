# REQ-CONTENT-010 — Eliminate Hedging Copy Site-Wide

**Category:** CONTENT / AUTHORITY  
**Priority:** CRITICAL  
**Phase:** 12  
**Status:** BACKLOG  

## Problem

Hedging language undermines authority throughout the site. MC claims Weapons School rigor and TOPGUN graduates but the copy sounds apologetic. Examples:

- "Three tools at various stages of maturity. Each one exists because we needed it."
- "We're building a studio that co-founds companies... We're still early — learning alongside the founders"
- "We're growing this alongside the community"
- "We're learning how to compress the gap... and getting faster each time"

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Audit all pages for hedging phrases ("we're early", "we're learning", "we're building", "still", "growing")
2. Replace each with direct, confident statements grounded in evidence
3. Voice profile compliance: thesis-driven, decisive, founder-selective
4. No self-deprecation that isn't honest reckoning (per voice profile: admit a flaw with specifics, don't hedge broadly)

## Evidence (Critical Assessment 2026-05-16)

"Are you confident in what you're building or aren't you?" — hedging reads as first-time founder pitching seed round, not operator-grade venture studio.

## Scope

Homepage, About, Programs, Portfolio — all top-funnel pages.
