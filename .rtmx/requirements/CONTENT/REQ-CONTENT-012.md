# REQ-CONTENT-012 — Clarify Logo Section Relationships

**Category:** CONTENT / HONESTY  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  
**Phase:** 12  
**Status:** BACKLOG  

## Problem

Social proof logos (INDOPACOM, DIU, AFSOC, etc.) with "Built with and delivered for" + "Organizations our team has worked inside — not conference introductions." This conflates employment/service history with customer/partner relationships. Sophisticated readers will question whether INDOPACOM hired MC or whether a founder served there in uniform.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Separate logos into clear categories: "Served in" vs "Delivered for" vs "Partnered with"
2. OR replace with a single honest framing: "Our team brings operational experience from:" (service/employment history)
3. If any are actual customer relationships (contract delivery), call that out distinctly
4. Remove any logo where the relationship is solely "attended a conference there"

## Evidence (Critical Assessment 2026-05-16)

"Did INDOPACOM hire you, or did you serve there in uniform? Those are very different things."
