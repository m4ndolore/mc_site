# REQ-CONTENT-013 — Business Model Transparency

**Category:** CONTENT / TRANSPARENCY  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  
**Phase:** 12  
**Status:** BACKLOG  

## Problem

The site never explains how MC makes money. No pricing, no economics, no "How we work" section. Equity? Fees? Subscriptions? If a sophisticated reader can't figure out the business model in 30 seconds, they assume there isn't one.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Add a "How we work" or "Economics" section (About page or dedicated section)
2. Explain the model clearly: co-founding equity, subscription tiers, marketplace fees (as applicable)
3. Doesn't need to be a full pricing page — a 2-3 sentence explanation is sufficient
4. Appears on a page reachable within 1 click from homepage

## Evidence (Critical Assessment 2026-05-16)

"If I can't figure out your business model in 30 seconds, I assume you don't have one."
