# REQ-CONTENT-011 — Attribute or Remove Hero Stats

**Category:** CONTENT / CREDIBILITY  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Phase:** 12  
**Status:** BACKLOG  

## Problem

Homepage hero stats ("83 Companies Validated · 21 Funded for Follow-On · 5 Partner Nations Engaged") are unattributed. "Validated how? Funded by whom? Which nations?" The funnel math is opaque — selected ≠ validated ≠ funded.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Each stat links to evidence or is contextualized inline
2. "83 Companies Validated" → clarify this is Combine Cohort 25-1 attendance, link to cohort page
3. "21 Funded for Follow-On" → name the metric (advanced to finals? received funding post-Combine? from whom?)
4. "5 Partner Nations Engaged" → name the nations or remove
5. If stats cannot be attributed with specifics, replace with verifiable claims

## Evidence (Critical Assessment 2026-05-16)

Big claims with zero attribution erode trust with sophisticated audiences (VCs, operators, founders who do diligence).
