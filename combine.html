The Combine

5-day operator integration cycle. Problem validation, team formation, prototype scoping.

Program Overview

Phase 01

The Combine is an intensive 5-day integration cycle where defense builders work directly with operators to validate problems, form teams, and scope prototypes. Participants gain direct access to mission owners and leave with validated requirements for venture formation in Merge Studio.

Day 1

Orientation

  • Operator briefings
  • Problem set review
  • Team assignments
Day 2

Problem Validation

  • Operator interviews
  • Constraint mapping
  • Requirements capture
Day 3

Solution Design

  • Architecture sessions
  • Feasibility analysis
  • Risk assessment
Day 4

Prototype Scoping

  • MVP definition
  • Timeline planning
  • Resource mapping
Day 5

Validation Review

  • Operator feedback
  • Go/no-go decisions
  • Studio selection

Participant Requirements

Security clearance eligibility or existing clearance preferred. Dual-use teams considered.
Technical founder or deep domain expertise in relevant capability area.
Ability to operate in Indo-Pacific theater. Travel to San Francisco required.
Commitment to 5-day intensive program. Full attendance required.
