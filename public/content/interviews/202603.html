The Capstone Project

Gathering resident feedback to improve outcomes.

The Capstone is the throughline from your residency to a tangible outcome for your organization: one clearly defined deliverable that can influence a decision, accelerate a capability, or create measurable value. This short form gathers your feedback to shape it — what you’ve learned so far, what’s working, and where the strongest opportunity lies. Where a field is prefilled, it’s a starting point; confirm it, sharpen it, or replace it.

Section A

Section D

Define the Deliverable

Turn the value into something concrete and finishable.

D1 · The deliverable

State it in one sentence: "By the end of the residency, I will deliver ___ that enables ___ to make a decision about ___." Edit the prefilled draft below.

D2 · Audience & decision

D3 · Evidence requirement

What evidence makes this credible? Mark what applies, then note specifics.

D4 · Transition potential

D5 · Minimum viable deliverable

Smallest version you could finish and defend by the final presentation — and the stretch version.
