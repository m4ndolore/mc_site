Survey Structure (10–12 Questions Total)
Section 1: Impact + Experience
* On a scale of 1–5, how valuable was SigmaBlox for your company or mission?
* What specific outcome did you achieve during or after SigmaBlox? (e.g., partnership, funding, prototype, new customer, insight, contact)
* What was the single most valuable part of the program for you?
* If you could change one thing about SigmaBlox, what would it be?
Section 2: Network + Momentum
* How many new partnerships or collaborations have emerged from SigmaBlox?
* Did you connect with potential funders or contracting officers? (Y/N)
* Estimate total new funding or opportunities in motion since attending (range options).
* Are you still in contact with your Pod coaches or other participants? (Y/N)
Section 3: Strategic Outcomes (KPI Collection)
We’re collecting this data to quantify mission and market impact — not to share externally without permission.
* Did SigmaBlox accelerate your path to funding or fielding? (Yes/No/Too early to tell)
* What is your current TRL (Technology Readiness Level) and how has it changed since?
* Has your engagement with DOD or primes increased post-event? (Yes/No)
* Estimate new jobs created or planned due to growth since SigmaBlox.
* Any measurable change in revenue, pipeline, or valuation post-event?
Section 4: Open Commentary
* Share a quote or testimonial about your SigmaBlox experience. (We’ll use select quotes—with your approval—for future publications.)
* What do you need most right now? (Options: funding, contracting support, testing access, team talent, introductions, etc.)
* Would you attend or recommend another Combine? (Yes/No/Maybe)
Section 5: Follow-Up + Visibility
* Would you be open to a short 15-minute follow-up interview or feature? (Yes/No)
* Drop your LinkedIn or contact info if you’d like to stay looped into the next round.
KPI Capture Framework (for Reporting Upstream)
When the results come in, you’ll be able to quantify:
* # of attendees (by category): founders, operators, SMEs, investors, acquisition
* # of ventures reviewed, selected, and scored
* $ value of gov capital surfaced / accelerated
* # of partnerships, contracts, or pilots launched
* # of warfighter needs validated or refined
* # of new introductions, collaborations, or follow-up actions created
* Testimonials + sentiment metrics (positive/neutral/negative)
