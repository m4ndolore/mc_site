Topic: Government Stakeholder Logistical Support
Background: Hadron was unsuccessful at facilitating a visit access request to enter the 613th
AOC before our arrival. Individual members of PACAF and the 613th are excited about the

security, reserving conference space, or briefing leadership about our arrival.

two week turn time for the Visit Access Request (VAR) for the AOC to get through legal and
contracting offices.
Recommendation: Please ask the government to assign a government sponsor to the
contracting company to interface with security and leadership. Educate companies on the VAR
process.
