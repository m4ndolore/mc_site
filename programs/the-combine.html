The Week

Five days to ground truth.

No innovation theater. No panels. Every session is with operators close to the requirement — the people who decide what gets fielded.

Day 1–2
Problem Immersion
Mission owners brief the operational context, constraints, and urgency behind each problem set. You hear the pain from the people who live it.
Day 3–4
Operator Feedback
Present directly to operators. Structured, candid feedback on mission fit, deployment path, and what would need to change for them to fight with it.
Day 5
Match Decisions
Go or no-go. Warm introductions to program offices, deployment partners, and capital. Every team leaves with a clear answer.
Eligibility

This is for builders with edge, not decks.

If you have a working product and the conviction to hear unfiltered operator feedback, you belong here. We select for technical credibility and mission relevance.

  • Funded or revenue-generating with a working product — not a slide deck
  • Technology aligned to Indo-Pacific operational problem sets
  • Ready to commit one week, in person — no remote, no observers
  • Ready for candid operator feedback — the kind that changes your roadmap

Earlier stage? Tell us what you're building and we'll help you find the right path.
