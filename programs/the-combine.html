The Week

One Week. Operator Feedback. Clear Next Steps.

The Combine compresses months of discovery into five days of focused validation with the people who own the problem.

Day 1–2
Problem Immersion
Deep-dive sessions with mission owners. Understand the operational context, constraints, and urgency behind each problem set.
Day 3–4
Solution Demos & Feedback
Present your technology directly to operators. Get candid, structured feedback on fit, feasibility, and deployment path.
Day 5
Match Decisions
Final presentations, match decisions, and next-step commitments. Leave with a clear path or a clear answer.
Eligibility

Who Should Apply

The Combine is for teams with real technology and real traction. Leave your slide deck at home.

  • Funded or revenue-backed teams with a working product
  • Emerging tech aligned to Indo-Pacific problem sets
  • Founders and builders ready to commit to one week, in person.
  • Teams ready for direct, unfiltered operator feedback

Not funded or ready yet? Join Defense Builders to meet fellow builders and validate problems with end users.
