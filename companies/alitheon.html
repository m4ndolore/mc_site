Overview

Supply Chain Integrity is at risk due to counterfeit parts and lack of reliable tracing. E.g. a million F-35 parts lost and fake computer boards installed in F-18s, B-52s. Why? DoD relies on easily compromised proxy barcodes, data plates, and tags.

FeaturePrint is software that serializes items just by taking a picture from a COTS camera, even a phone. What fingerprints are for people FeaturePrints are for things.

It is deployed with commercial customers across sectors. In DoD achieved 100% accuracy in tests incl. 2 USAF and 1 Army Phase II for inventory management and anticounterfeiting.

TRL In commercial production. DoD TRL 7 - testing use cases and need ATO.

Strategic Advantage No proxies; COTS cameras and phones; 59 patents. Zero trust for physical items.

Go-to-Market Access SBIR Phase I and II; Tradwinds

Dual-Use - Currently deployed with luxury goods, collectibles, art, healthcare, automotive and aviation, and precious metals.

Team - 20 experienced members incl. Roei Ganzarski, CEO, 30 years of business, software, and aerospace; Mark Tocci, 30 years of software, Multithreading, Big Data, Algorithms, Pattern Recognition Dr. Dave Stevens, 30 years of advanced mathematics, ML, and speech recognition.

Competitive Landscape Only other company is German-based Bosch. Alitheon is only one with issued patents.

Primary User Quartermasters, inventory leads, supply chain leads, service and maintenance leads.

User-Critical Problem - Supply chain - can get duped by fake products that reduce mission readiness and lethality, and increase costs. We ensure products are authentic, verified, and validated. - Inventory - takes multiple warfighters to manage inventory manually = takes a long time and mistakes are made. We reduce resources and time by 90% with no mistakes.
