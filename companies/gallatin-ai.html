Gallatin AI

Navigator
Alumni Logistics Multi Software TRL 6 Series Funding 20-50 people

Overview

Problem Today's logisticians suffer from fragmented logistics common operating pictures, inaccurate demand forecasting, and highly manual sustainment planning, hobbling our ability to make decisions at the speed of relevance to large scale combat.

Solution Navigator is an AI decision platform that includes an intuitive logistics common operating picture, a demand forecasting module and a resupply course of action generation engine.

Field Validation Fielding across Army units in 2025.

Technology Maturity (TRL) TRL 6 forecasting achieving TRL 7 by the end of 2025 after several field training exercises.

Strategic Advantage Our technology is designed to continuously improve with use, creating network effects. Our team includes several experienced defense tech leaders. We maintain a network of retired military logisticians who inform every aspect of development.

Go-to-Market Access Currently have a SBIR and corporate partnerships with several primes

Dual-Use Potential We made our first commercial sale for logistics decision support in emergency response.

Team Co-Founder/CEO - Formerly Scale AI federal leader and Palantir engineer. Co-Founder/CTO - Pioneer of drone delivery, including standing up Amazon Prime Air. Co-Founder/CPO - Founded a dual-use logistics tech company that exited via acquisition

Competitive Landscape Within the Army, many units have seen many solutions and assess Gallatin as the strongest.

Primary User Logistics cells of tactical through theater HQs (S4, G4, A4, J4) and planners within logistics units (AF LRS', Army TSC's, etc.). We have two distinct product areas, segmented by tactical logistics and theater logistics.

User-Critical Problem Today’s logistics workflows will not scale to meet the demands of near-peer conflict.
