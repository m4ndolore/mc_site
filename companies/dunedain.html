Dunedain

Warmind
Alumni Command and Control Multi Both TRL 6 10-20 people

Overview

Problem Units burn critical hours on mapping, monitoring OSINT feeds, conducting MDMP, and checking doctrine from SOPs - in the fight this will often need to be offline and in a contested, high OPTEMP environment.

Solution Warmind is a hybrid cloud / air-gappable AI platform that unifies geospatial analysis, OSINT ingestion, S1 / S2 / S3 artifact generation including MDMP products, and COP export into one portable system.

Field Validation A prototype system has been tested with 19th SFG(A) in force on force exercises leading to a reported 400% acceleration in planning (orders, WARNO production and AAR synthesis). Multiple units that have ordered our system for purchase. Our unclassified open beta is also live now at beta.dunedainsystems.com with hundreds of monthly active users.

TRL TRL 6 – Demonstrated prototype in real exercises and lab integration with TAK/COP tools.

Strategic Advantage Deep doctrinal integration, modular unit and user-specific data retrieval, proprietary agent orchestration pipeline finetuned on Army data and built specifically for the Warfighter.

Go-to-Market GPC-purchasable Warmind Laptops

Team -Mack Ohlinger, CEO: West Point ’15, LBS MBA ’22, ex-Army Captain -Matt Kane, CTO: West Point '15, Johns Hopkins MSE, former Signal Officer

Competitive Landscape Unlike Camo GPT, Warmind is fully air-gapped and offers end-to-end agent workflows plus a flexible retrieval database enabling persistent unit and user memory enabling superior knowledge management and workflow outputs.

Primary User S3 planners and S2 intel analysts who need rapid COP creation, rapid and accurate orders drafting and route planning in DDIL environments.

User-Critical Impact Warmind delivers a ready-to-use operational artifacts in minutes, replacing fragmented tools and laborious manual staff-work associated with COP generation, staff work and other MDMP tasks.
