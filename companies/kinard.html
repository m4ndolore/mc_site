Overview

Problem sUAS are a lethal tactical problem that are also strategic defeat threat. Warfighters, assets and infrastructure are vulnerable to asymmetric attack & lack the capability to defend themselves

Solution Kinetic, hard-kill point defense for individuals, vehicles & assets to defend from sUAS and FPVs

TRL TRL 6. Tested in a relevant environment, subsystems and total system.

Strategic Advantage Build and integrate hardware, software, and munitions systems, tailor them better than competitors only working one piece of the puzzle. 2 patents plus 1 pending.

Go-to-Market Access Customers & end-users with identified pain point. Demonstrations (USAFE, AFSOC), CRADA development (DEVCOM, RAID), exercises (Cobra Gold 26), SBIR D2P2 submitted, submissions for AAL CLPS RFP.

Dual-Use Potential Infrastructure, energy, and large gatherings vulnerable to UAS and aware of their vulnerability.

Developed SOF cWMD technologies. Built multiple teams to 9 figure exits Mike Blout - Head of Business Development - Retired AF Combat Controller. Special Warfare Prep Course Superintendent, AF Dive School Superintendent, business development experience across SOF and conventional

Competitive Landscape ACS, Zeromark, Anduril. We protect everything & everyone they don't
