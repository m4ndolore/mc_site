Overview

Current ISR platforms have limited endurance and limited loiter capability curtailing intelligence gathering capabilities and providing adversaries with evasion opportunities.

Anuma's patented Partial-Vacuum Lift (PVL) enabled platforms have unprecedented endurance and loiter capability - potential for months-long deployment rather than hours or days.

Patent issued 2023. What we're doing is incredibly difficult and will be very hard to duplicate without violating our patent.

Our beachhead market is commercial weather data and we're developing our first platform with Phase I and Phase II SBIR grants from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Association.

Weather data acquisition, border and coastal surveillance, logistics, wildfire surveillance

Jamie Little, Cofounder and CEO, aerospace engineer, project management professional (PMP), 35+ years of experience, serial entrepreneur Diana Little, Cofounder and President, computer scientist, data analyst, 35+ years of experience, serial entrepreneur Dave Craft, Chief of Staff, former CEO of a successful startup, 35+ years of experience in R&D, technology transfer, and corporate acquisitions

Heavier-than-air (HTA) aircraft require the continuous expenditure of energy to stay aloft. Our platforms are lighter-than-air (LTA), meaning they are buoyant and low-energy. All other LTA platforms use consumable helium. Our platforms use partial-vacuum, which is infinitely renewable, and provide them with unprecedented endurance and loiter capabilities, as well as enhanced maneuverability, altitude control, safer ground handling, and lower operational cost.

Reconnaissance squadrons

Keeping ISR assets in the air where they can do the most good for C2 and the warfighter.
