Alumni Maintenance Multi Software TRL 7 Grant Funded 10-20 people

Overview

Problem In 2025 mission capable rates for aircraft fell to their lowest in recent memory: 62%

A surprising contributor to this was paper. Not even physical paper, but its digital counterparts like PDFs. Despite being digital, the knowledge in the diagrams, tables, charts and instructions within are still not easily accessible from software systems.

Solution Redshred provides an API-first document AI platform for digital transformation and data readiness. that lets you dial in exactly what you need from these documents and turn it into the structured data you need to power downstream applications. Redshred lets you treat your documents as a database.

Field Validation Commercial customers; Air Mobility Command; DAF Rapid Sustainment Office; USMC PM AAA; Navy FRCSW

Technology Maturity (TRL) TRL 7+

Strategic Advantage Patents and patents pending; subject matter expertise in aviation maintenance.

Go-to-Market Access SBIRs including AFWERX, DARPA, Navy, and Army and is on the RO-MAC IDIQ and Tradewinds.

Dual-Use Potential Commercial MRO and engineering; digital transformation across an array of industries

Team Redshred's founders have run multiple businesses and have a diverse past including prior startup engineering experience at Sourcefire. Team includes multiple PhDs, software engineers, and data scientists as well as maintenance subject matter experts.

Competitive Landscape Manual conversion, bespoke software development, OCR companies and general purpose LLMs like ChatGPT. Redshred’s unique advantage is choreographing many layers of AI, synthesizing their results, and providing a database-like interface via APIs.

Primary User Maintainers and engineers.

User-Critical Problem Modernized access to tech data supporting maintenance training and operations.
