Overview

Problem Any warfighter making decisions within a large combinatorial situation...and most military decisions involve large combinatorial situations...is either lucky or wrong. This is because no classical tool exists for delivering complex multi-variate solutions in real-time.

Solution Our desktop-based, quantum optimization interface replaces spreadsheet (or even whiteboard) heuristics, estimations, approximations and guesses.

Field Validation Our technology is Phase III and in production operation with AFMC.

Technology Maturity (TRL) TRL9 (in use for AFMC at Tinker AFB).

Strategic Advantage Patented

Go-to-Market Access 5 SBIR/STTR

Dual-Use Potential We have commercial interest. We have 1 prime partner.

Team We have 2 of the nation's top physicists, 1 active-duty AF physicist (TDY) and several Purdue University top Computer Scientist programmers.

Competitive Landscape There are 3 other US-based SBC's that are delivering quantum software. However, we are the leader in the DoD space and the only company to have quantum software in (non-R&D) production.

Primary User Inventory managers and battle managers

User-Critical Problem Anyone that needs to prioritize multiple choices across multiple constrains can leverage our technology.
