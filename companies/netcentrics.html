NetCentrics Corporation

Wraith
Alumni Cyber Cyber Software TRL 6 20-50 people

Overview

Problem Mission teams in distributed, contested, hybrid, or classified environments lack real-time, enriched cyber visibility—causing delayed threat detection, analyst overload, and degraded mission assurance. Solution Wraith is a cloud-native telemetry fusion and enrichment platform delivering mission-critical insights in disconnected, denied, hybrid, and distributed environments. Field Validation Deployed with the Air National Guard, supporting two Cyber Protection Teams (CPTs). Technology Maturity (TRL) TRL 7 — Operational in DoD, including JWICS/SIPR, supporting ANG CPT missions. Strategic Advantage Leverages NetCentrics’ classified access to ingest unique telemetry, integrates with Sentinel and Splunk, and operates where traditional tools fail—disconnected and multi-domain. Go-to-Market Access DIU via SigmaBlox, NetCentrics IDIQs (DISA, INDOPACOM, USCG, VA), SBIR, and SOC/SOAR roles. Dual-Use Potential Energy (OT/ICS telemetry), Logistics (contested edge), and AI Safety (model monitoring). Team Kenny Cushing (CEO), Steve Wieland (CTO), Ashish Francis (VP Data/AI), Brian Dutcher (Chief Architect), plus Chaney, Long, Fecteau, Nalls—experts from USAF, Guard, USCYBERCOM, OSD R&E, and Intel. Competitive Landscape Unlike Vectra, MixMode, Leidos, or Booz Allen, Wraith spans classification levels (NIPR/SIPR/JWICS), functions offline, and enriches telemetry pre-SIEM/SOAR. Primary User Cyber analysts, SOC leads, and operators in classified/multi-domain ops. User-Critical Problem Analysts face siloed, noisy telemetry and offline gaps. Wraith fuses reliable insights across cloud and on-prem—without persistent connectivity.
