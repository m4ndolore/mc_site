Nighthawk Integrated Solutions LLC

OverWatch Locator (OWL)
Alumni Force Protection (Defense) Multi Hardware TRL 6 3-5 people

Overview

Problem OverWatch Locator (OWL) was initially designed for firefighters operating in the austere fire conditions inside structures such as high thermal temperatures, zero visibility from smoke, structural collapse, etc.

Solution OWL is a deployable 3D interior tracking system designed around ultra wideband (UWB) to dramatically reduce the number of deaths/severe injuries across the community by reducing the exposure of firefighters through decreasing search & rescue time from minutes to seconds.

Field Validation Field tested multiple times. Full scale tech demonstrated October 2023.

Tech Maturity (TRL) TRL 6

Strategic Advantage The tracking tech that powers OWL is a first of its kind deployable 3D tracking system. Patented in: USA (3 issued, 1 pending), Canada (1 issued), European Union (1 pending).

Go-to-Market Access AFWERX, AFRL, DHS S&T.

Dual-Use Potential From the beginning, OWL has been designed to meet or exceed MIL SPEC to allow for use in DoD with no changes to the tech.

Team Phil Taber is a 22-year USAF combat veteran, USAF Weapons School Graduate (Top Gun), squadron commander, operational test evaluator and was inducted into the USAF Safety Hall of Fame.

Competitive Landscape The primary competitor in the market space is Ascent Integrated Tech and uses an iPhone 16 "Find My Phone" style app for tracking. Does not work in high thermal temperatures environments.

Primary User First Responders (firefighters, law enforcement), Small Teams (SOF, FBI HRT, SWAT, JTAC, etc.), Ships (interior/exterior), Underground Facilities (Blue Personnel Tracking), Subterrain Ops (UGF and tunnel complexes), hospitals, etc.

User-Critical Problem Interior tracking is the Holy Grail is firefighting tech and is desperately needed save lives and reduce severe injuries.
