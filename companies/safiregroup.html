Safire Technology Group

Safire Products & Systems with Safer and High Density Batteries
Alumni Force Protection (Defense) Multi Both TRL 7 Angel / Private 10-20 people

Overview

Problem Warfighters are forced to rely on underperforming batteries and fragmented energy infrastructure. This results in logistics complexity and increased exposure to threats from both the enemy and the elements.

Solution Stratos Battery Pack: a modular, ruggedized, and safety-enhanced energy solution designed for seamless platform and system integration. Safire platforms include mobile energy storage trailers, renewable microgrid energy storage, electric dirt bikes, and extreme cold weather unmanned robotics. Stratos packs collect real-time battery data—feed it into SafireEOS, our advanced energy operating system. This data layer empowers real-time decision making and AI-driven logistics modeling to prevent failures and enhance operational readiness.

Field Validation Stratos battery pack and electric dirt bike tested by 3 ASOS in field environment. Mobile and stationary storage prototypes at pre-field levels.

Technology Maturity (TRL) TRL 7 for key products through user field testing.

Strategic Advantage Battery cell and pack safety IP, power electronics IP with exclusive data acess.

Go-to-Market Access AFWERX SBIRs, TACFI, and OTA contract.

Dual-Use Potential Energy, energy management, and electric vehicles / UxVs.

Team Safire is a team of experts in materials science, battery engineering, and systems integration from organizations such as Palantir, Tesla, and SOCOM.

Competitive Landscape Other companies solving this problem use outdated battery chemistries, limited safety features, and lack electronics intelligence

Primary User JTACs, CCTs, tactical operators, force protection, maintenance, expeditionary airfield establishment.

User-Critical Problem More widespread and modular access to higher performing energy systems enables Agile Combat Employment.
