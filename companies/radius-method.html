Overview

Mission software teams struggle with speed. While AI offers acceleration, DoD programs lack secure, compliant deployment for autonomous workflows, especially in IL5+ and disconnected environments. The issue isn't model access, but operationalizing them securely and repeatedly across the full software lifecycle. Crystal Tower solves this. It's a secure, AI-native software factory enabling governed, provable, and deployable agent workflows that boost developer output 10–20x, transforming DevSecOps into secure cognition. Already fielded in 5GAT, PEO-STRI UnITE, and Kessel Run, Crystal Tower supports govt and vendor teams delivering critical capabilities today. With a TRL-9, it's deployed in live environments, driving outcomes, meeting compliance and Zero Trust. Crystal Tower's foundation—a ModSecOps framework for agent governance, prompt control, and cognitive auditability, integrated into hardened IL4+ pipelines—makes it hard to replicate. This, plus early traction through SBIR, AFWERX, and leading primes, gives Radius Method a durable lead. The platform extends to energy, AI safety, and autonomous logistics, where secure, accountable automation is crucial. Radius Method’s team brings firsthand commercial and government expertise from Platform One, Kessel Run, TRMC, and PEO STRI, having deployed platforms where others failed. While others focus on tools or model access, Crystal Tower delivers operational agent workflows with built-in governance, fielded today. Our users are software leads, test teams, and DevOps engineers in classified and tactical programs. Their reality: brittle pipelines, long compliance delays, and inability to integrate AI. Crystal Tower replaces this with secure, compliant, agent-enabled workflows, accelerating delivery without compromising control.
