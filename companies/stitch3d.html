Stitch3D

Stitch3D
Alumni Intel and Battlespace Awareness Multi Information Software TRL 9 3-5 people

Overview

Problem Traditional airfield inspections takes >8 hours; modern 3D laser scanning reduces capture to <1 hour. Yet LiDAR data remain difficult to access due to fragmented storage and outdated desktop software, delaying time-sensitive decisions in airfield recovery, terrain, and infrastructure assessments

Solution Stitch3D is a cloud-native platform making 3D geospatial data usable on the web. Upload, visualize, analyze, and collaborate on 3D data with up to 50 users in real time, streamlining terrain workflows for faster, mission-critical decisions

Field Validation Field-tested with 6 CES, 627 CES, and 628 CES. At JBLM, 627 CES used Stitch3D to share 1,000+ acres of drone LiDAR vegetation obstruction data with AMC A4, TERPS, 62 OSS, AFCEC, and the Army DPW

Technology Maturity (TRL) TRL 9. Commercial launch Jan 2024; MRR growing 25% MoM

Strategic Advantage Our proprietary pipeline converts any LiDAR format into universal web format. Integrated hosting, visualization, and analysis tools in a powerful yet simple UI

Go-to-Market Access 7 total SBIR/STTR; signed MFR/LOS from 627 CES, 628 ABW, and AFCEC command leadership

Dual-Use Potential Stitch3D serves 1,000+ commercial drone service providers across construction, utilities, and real estate

Team Clark Yuan, CEO - former US Army intel officer. USMA Engineering; MIT and Harvard MBA/MPA Jeff Jakinovich, CTO - former senior software engineer at Bosch

Competitive Landscape Unlike ESRI and DroneDeploy, Stitch3D is cloud-native, intuitive, and LiDAR-capable at scale

Primary User GeoMobility Coordinator, AMC/A4C; Geospatial Integration Manager, AFCEC/CBFG; PACAF Geospatial Integration Officer (GIO)

User-Critical Problem Manual inspections strain CE teams. 3D models cut time, boost risk visibility, and optimize manpower
